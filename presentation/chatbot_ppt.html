Project Overview

  • AI-powered chatbot for medical information retrieval and assistance
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture for accurate responses
  • Safe, context-based answers grounded in verified medical documents

Problem Statement

  • Unreliable online sources - People search medical info online but get conflicting, unverified answers
  • Misinformation risk - Incorrect self-diagnosis can lead to harmful health decisions
  • Need for trust - A trustworthy assistant using verified medical documents is essential

Our Solution

  • Verified knowledge base - Chatbot answers only from uploaded medical PDFs
  • Advanced AI pipeline - AI + embeddings + vector search for precise retrieval
  • Safety first - Doctor-consultation disclaimer included in every response
